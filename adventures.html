Nobody told Lola what was coming. One minute she was napping, the next she was in the tub. She gave her family the most betrayed look imaginable the entire time. Full drama. Zero chill. Ten out of ten performance.

Lola is not a car ride enthusiast. She spent the whole trip with her chin resting on the armrest, staring out the window with a worried expression. Every bump in the road was treated as a personal attack.
